Dan Bythewood Jr.
President and Managing Partner, La Cité Development

Syed Hasan
President, Springer International.

"I have known François personally and professionally for well over a decade. His elegance, dedication and professionalism are complemented by an engaging and giving personality. RuthAnne Dreisbach
Chief Executive Officer, Dreisbach Group.

"Knowing François Belizaire is a true joy! He is kind, keen, professional and authentic. I have enjoyed discovering the talented brands that he collaborates with since I first met him at Hermes. François always keeps me updated on his clients' events and I feel his attention to details is what sets him apart. Francois is truly the best addition to any company for brand awareness."

Frederica Monaco
Founder + CEO, Frederica Monaco Interior Design

"Watching François over the years has been a joy to observe. He works diligently to make sure his high end customers are taken care of. I have seen him handle international brands with such care and orchestrate events that are not only elegant but that are timely and productive."

Phoenix K. Jackson
Founder & Chief Communications Officer of the Phoenix Effect

"François puts a lot of time, passion, and attention to detail into all the projects he takes on, focusing on delivering quality work. His natural ability for connecting with people and strategically forming collaborations within his network are truly a gift, and what sets François apart from the rest."

Salwa Owens
Chief Executive Creator and Founder.
