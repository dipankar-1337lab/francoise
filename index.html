About Francoise

My name is Francois WOLFF Belizaire.I was born the son of a music loving mathematician turened attorney, and undeniably, the best mom and chef ever.
Top producing and engaging Sales and Business Development Leader known for driving new sales revenue growth while retaining key client accounts for sustained profitability. Proven record of performance in Hospitality, Luxury Goods, Events and B2B Business Services industries. Established network of contacts in the field on a global scale comprised of Executives, High Net Worth Individuals, VIPs, and Celebrities. Works well with staff at all levels to include executive management, key decision makers, internal/external customers, and vendors for positive business and sales outcomes.
